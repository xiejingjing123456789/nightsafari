Night Safari Visitors

Problem Statement

In 2020, the world's first nocturnal zoo, the Night Safari in Singapore, attracted approximately 343 thousand visitors. The Night Safari is part of the four wildlife-centred leisure attractions in Singapore, which includes the:
  • Singapore Zoo
    • Zebra
    • Monkey
    • Polar Bear
  • Bird Paradise
    • Parrot
    • Owl
    • Eagle
  • Night Safari
    • Panda
    • Red Panda

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Night Safari was closed from April to June 2020, and has since then operated at a limited capacity under safety measures.
